About Me

Dineth Dilshan Gunawardana is a talented photographer and short film editor, known for his contributions to Istock and GettyImage. Born on March 20th, 2007 in Kaluthara, Sri Lanka, Dineth showed a passion for photography from an early age. He honed his skills while studying at Mahanama College Colombo and Kaluthara Vidyalaya Kaluthara. His journey into the world of visual storytelling and capturing moments began to flourish during his formative years at Mahanama College Colombo and Kaluthara Vidyalaya Kaluthara. It was in these educational settings that Dineth diligently honed his photography skills, developing a keen eye for detail and a unique perspective that would set him apart in the world of visual arts. Dineth's portfolio reflects a diverse range of interests, with a particular affinity for wildlife photography, capturing the intricate beauty of birds in their natural habitats. His lens also extends to the dynamic realm of street photography, where he skillfully captures the essence of urban life, showcasing the vibrancy and stories that unfold in the streets. Beyond his technical prowess, Dineth is known for his creative vision and the ability to evoke emotions through his work. His dedication to the craft has garnered recognition not only on local platforms but also on a global scale, making him a notable figure in the field of photography. As a multifaceted artist, Dineth continues to explore new avenues of expression, pushing the boundaries of his creativity. With each click of the shutter and frame edited, he weaves visual narratives that resonate with audiences worldwide, solidifying his presence in the ever-evolving landscape of visual arts. Dineth Dilshan Gunawardana's journey in photography is a testament to his unwavering passion, talent, and commitment to the art form.
